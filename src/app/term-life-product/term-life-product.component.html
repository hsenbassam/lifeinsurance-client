Term Life Insurance

The future holds many surprises, some of which might be unpleasant. This is why it is important to provide a financial protection coverage that will ensure financial independence to the family and maintain the same lifestyle for them.

The term life insurance plan from us is a conventional life insurance policy that provides the essential protection in case of death or disabilities.

Flexible protection options with various insurance covers and riders.

Possibility to choose the contract period according to your age and your insurance needs.

Flexibility to choose the renewability option to extend your coverage period.

Flexibility of payments (monthly / quarterly / half-yearly / yearly)

Natural or accidental death: A lump-sum amount paid to the beneficiaries of the insured in case of death.
  • Joint Life Option: for a spouse or business partner
  • Renewability: For policies with periods of 5 years, we grant you the ability to renew your policy with no additional medical requirements.
  • Total Permanent Disablement (TPD): In case of disablement, the main cover is paid in equal installments over 5 years.
  • Passive War Risk: A cover in times of war or war-like circumstances, granting you protection in case of death or total permanent disablement (as an added option).
  • For life insurance policies tied that are tied to an existing bank loan, we offer a Decreasing Term Life policy, allowing you to pay less premiums as you pay off the loan.
  • Min Entry Age : 18; Max Entry Age : 94
  • Coverage Term : from 15 to 35 years
  • Minimum Premium : USD 100K
